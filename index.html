Blackpink is a popular South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment. The group debuted on August 8, 2016, with four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They quickly gained international recognition and acclaim for their music, style, and performances. Blackpink's musical style blends various genres including pop, hip hop, and EDM, resulting in a dynamic and energetic sound. Their songs often feature catchy hooks, powerful choreography, and visually stunning music videos.
